Oscar

The Academy awards, also known as the Oscars, are awards for rewarding extraordinary merit within the film industry. The award is famously given to actors who excel at portraying certain roles.

Emmy

The Emmy award is used to recognize excellence within television. The award has many categories which can be divided into primetime and daytime shows and the categories for actors are divided into drama shows, comedy shows, and "limited series or movie".

Tony

The Antoinette Perry Award for Excellence in Broadway Theatre, or just the Tony award, is an award given for excellence within Broadway theatre. The award had many catergories, some of which awards actors within musicals and theatre.
